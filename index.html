The Realm

Dakoria doesn't ease you in.

You arrive with nothing — no crown, no camp, no one waiting on the other side of the tree line. What Dakoria gives you is a realm that keeps moving whether you're ready or not: light fades, hunger builds, and the things in the dark don't check if you've eaten.

Every run starts the same way and ends differently. What you gather, what you build, and what you decide is worth the risk — that's the whole game.

There's no one else to lean on here, and no one else to blame either. Dakoria is built to be survived alone.

How Survival Works

Every meter is something you have to manage yourself

There's no auto-heal and no one covering for you. What's above is close to what you'll actually see in-game — the numbers move because of what you do, not on a script.

  • Hunger & Thirst drop steadily — forage, hunt, or trade to keep them up.
  • Warmth depends on shelter and gear, especially once night falls.
  • Stamina limits how much you can push in a single stretch before you need rest.
  • Inventory is limited on purpose — every slot is a decision.
